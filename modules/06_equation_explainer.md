# Module 6: Equation Explainer

## Responsibilities
- Identify mathematical symbols:
  - Greek letters (Σ, θ, λ).
  - Operations (+, ×, ÷).
  - Exponents (^2, ^n).
- Extract all equations from paper.
- For each equation:
  - Provide a 1-sentence explanation of its purpose.

## Outputs
- List of equations.
- One-sentence explanation per equation.
